About Me

I live in the south eastern desert of New Mexico in a small town known for its cave, the Carlsbad Caverns. I have lived here all of my life, but I have been to 13 states and one other country besides this one. I enjoy many things, especially outdoor adventures such as mountain biking, hiking, swimming, and rock hounding. I became interested in web development a few years ago and it was mostly a fun hobby. I learned a little bit of HTML and CSS and then a couple months ago, I was offered a job with training to learn more and so here I am in Bootcamp.
